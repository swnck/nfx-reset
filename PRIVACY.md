# Privacy Policy for NFX Reset
Last Updated: April 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how **NFX Reset** ("the Extension") handles information. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and being transparent about how the extension operates.

## 2. No Data Collection
**NFX Reset does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other identifying information.
- **Browsing History**: We do not track the websites you visit or your browsing activity outside of the specific functionality required on Netflix.com.
- **Usage Data**: We do not use analytics or tracking scripts to monitor how you use the extension.

## 3. How the Extension Works
NFX Reset operates entirely within your local browser environment.
- **User Interface**: The extension injects a "Reset" button into the Netflix interface to improve user experience.
- **Functionality**: When you click the button, a local script interacts with the Netflix API to update the playback status of the selected title. This process happens directly between your browser and Netflix.
- **No External Servers**: No data is sent to the developer or any third-party servers.

## 4. Permissions
The extension requires the following permissions to function:
- **Host Permission (`https://www.netflix.com/*`)**: Needed solely to display the reset button on the Netflix website and perform the reset action.
- **Scripting**: Used to execute the logic that handles the button click.

## 5. Third-Party Services
While the extension interacts with Netflix, it does not share any data with other third parties. Your use of Netflix is governed by Netflix’s own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

## 7. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue on our official GitHub repository.
